[] Email Notification
You may configure the TeraStation to email you regular status reports. For this to work, you will need an accessable SMTP server.

[]Syslog
You may configure the TeraStation to send logs to a syslog server.

[]Alert Sound Settings
By default, the TeraStation beeps when it overheats, has a disk error, its fan stops turning, or the UPS loses power. You may disable some or all of these alerts.
[]Front Panel Settings
You may choose the brightness of the LCD display as well as what is displayed.
