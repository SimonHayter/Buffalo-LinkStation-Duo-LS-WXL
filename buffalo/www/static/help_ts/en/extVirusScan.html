[] Virus Scanning
Click on the name of a share to change virus scanning settings for the individual share. Realtime scans, scheduled scans, and manual scans may be disabled or enabled for each share. To use virus scanning, you will also need to create a quarantine folder and enable the virus scanning service below. To activate updates for virus scanning, you must purchase a license pack separately.

[] Virus Scan Service
By default, the virus scanning service is disabled on the TeraStation. Before enabling it, create a new folder on the TeraStation for quarantining virus files. For best results, the quarantine folder should be a hidden SMB folder accessable only by administrators.

[] Modify Settings
Click to enable (or disable) the virus scanning service and to select the quarantine folder.

[] Open Virus Scan WebUI
Click to activate or configure virus scanning, check virus logs, schedule updates, check proxy settings, or activate security updates.

[] Initialize Virus Scan Settings
Click to return all antivirus settings to their factory defaults.
