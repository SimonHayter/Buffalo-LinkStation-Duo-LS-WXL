[] Check Disk
Click [Check Disk] to test your hard disk for correct operation. This will not erase any data from your disk.

[] Format Disk
Click [Format Disk] to reformat your hard disk. All data on the drive will be lost. This cannot be undone!

[] RAID Scanning
You may configure a RAID scan to run automatically at regular intervals. Click [Modify Settings] to configure automatic RAID scans. This will not erase any data from your array.

[] RAID
Drives may be combined in RAID 0, RAID 1, RAID 5, or RAID 10 arrays. Click [Change RAID] to change your RAID settings. Changing to a different type of RAID will erase all data on the array. All data on the drives in the RAID array will be lost. This cannot be undone!
