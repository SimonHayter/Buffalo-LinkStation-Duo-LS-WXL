[] Groups
A list of groups on the device is displayed. To create a new group, click [Create Group].
To edit a group, click on its group name, and a screen to edit group properties opens.
To delete any group, select (check) the group and click [Delete].

[] Add New Group
Group Names may have up to 12 alphanumeric characters. Letters, numbers, `-`(hyphen), `_`(under score) and `.`(dot) may be used. Symbols (except for `_`(under score)) cannot be used as the first character.
Group Descriptions may have up to 50 alphanumeric characters, or up to 25 two-byte characters. Letters, numbers, two byte characters, `-`(hyphen), `_`(under score), and ` `(space) may be used. Symbols, except for `-`(hyphen) and `_`(under score) cannot be used as the first character in a group description.

[] Quotas
You may enter both hard or soft limits for groups in GB. If If a user exceeds his group's soft limit, he will receive a warning but is allowed to continue until he hits the hard limit. The soft limit should always be smaller than the hard limit.
