[] Name

Enter a 'TeraStation Name' to identify your TeraStation on the network. Up to 12 alphanumeric characters, -(hyphen), and _(underscore) can be used. Symbols may not be used.

You may also enter a description to distinguish this TeraStation on the network. Up to 50 alphanumeric characters can be used in the description. Alphanumeric characters, '-'(hyphen), '_'(underscore) and ' '(space) can be used. Symbols cannot be used. The Description will be visible from Windows computers only. It will not be visible from computers running Mac OS.

[] Date and Time

The TeraStation's time may be set automatically or manually.

Choose [Automatic] to use an NTP time server to synchronize your time settings automatically. You may specify the synchronization frequency. To use a specific NTP server, enter its DNS name (FQDN) or IP address, such as ntp.jst.mfeed.ad.jp or 192.168.11.123, or use the default NTP time server.

Alternately, you may enter the time and date manually.
When done, click [Save] to save your settings.

Click [Use local time] to set the TeraStation's clock using your PC's system time.
Note: The system clock in the TeraStation may lose synchronization over a long period. For best results, use NTP to update the system time automatically.
