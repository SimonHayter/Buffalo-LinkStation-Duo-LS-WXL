[] BitTorrent
TeraStation’s built-in BitTorrent client will download your files for you. To enable it, click [Enable]. Choose a target folder for downloaded files to go to and click [Save]. Then, click [Open Download Manager].

[] Adding Torrents
To add a torrent, click [Add] and then [Browse]. Navigate to the torrent file and open it. The file(s) will automatically download in the background.
