[] UPS Settings

You may choose how your TeraStation behaves when connected to an Uninteruptable Power Supply. Click [Modify Settings] to change these settings.

Notes:
- Some of LinkStation models does not support UPS Settings.

[] Sleep Timer

You may configure up to three situations where the TeraStation powers down for a set period.

For example: To have the TeraStation enter sleep mode every day between noon and 3 pm, set a timer with 'Daily' as the timer interval, 12:00 as the 'Begin Sleep' time, and 15:00 as the 'Wake Up' time.
