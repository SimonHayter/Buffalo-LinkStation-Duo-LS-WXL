[] Disk Check
Click [Disk Check] to test your hard disk for correct operation. This will not erase any data from your disk.

[] Disk Format
Click [Disk Format] to reformat your hard disk. All data on the drive will be lost. This cannot be undone!

[] Disk Erase
Click [Disk Format] to erase all data from your hard disk. All data on the drive will be lost. This cannot be undone!
