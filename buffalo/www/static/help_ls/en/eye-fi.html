[] Configuring Eye-Fi

In the LinkStation's web admin interface, navigate to [Extensions] > [Eye-Fi connected]. Enter your Eye-Fi email address and password. Click on your Eye-Fi card from the list. [Enable] Transfer Locally and choose a destination folder. All files from your Eye-Fi card (including jpeg, raw, and video files) will now be transferred to this folder automatically.

[] Changing Eye-Fi Destinations

After configuring Eye-Fi on the LinkStation, all files on the Eye-Fi card will be copied to the selected folder on the LinkStation automatically. If you change the distination folder for some types of files in an Eye-Fi manager in another device (such as your computer), and then go back into the LinkStation's Eye-Fi interface to make further changes, you'll get a "Multiple Destinations" message. Choose [Partial Transfer] to leave the multiple destination settings in place, or [Full Transfer] to reconfigure the Eye-Fi card to transfer all files, regardless of type, to the selected share on the LinkStation.
